Access to the originals:
Original materials are accessible through the University Library's Special Collections. The Library Search facility http://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/ records thousands of medical texts but, until re-cataloguing is complete, researchers may need to check the Medical Library card catalogue. Consultation is free of charge but appointments must be made and proof of identity must be shown.
