Privacy

This privacy notice was last updated on 24 May 2018.

An overview of how we process personal data for Historical Texts.

The information on this page supplements Jisc's standard privacy notice.

Example 1: An individual gives feedback on the Historical Texts service using the survey tool and we are processing personal data

By providing information to the Historical Texts feedback survey you agree that you have asked us to process it as described in our standard privacy notice (at https://www.jisc.ac.uk/website/privacy-notice). You may instruct us to stop processing it at any time by e-mailing historicaltexts@jisc.ac.uk or help@jisc.ac.uk with your email address: this will remove your data from future statistics and reports.

Example 2: A member of the Historical Texts Advisory Board - http://historicaltexts.jisc.ac.uk/advisoryboard

The following information is needed for us to communicate with you as a member of the Historical Texts Advisory Board. We’ll use it, as described in our standard privacy notice (at https://www.jisc.ac.uk/website/privacy-notice), to provide the service you’ve requested, as well as to identify problems or ways to make the service better. We'll keep the information until we are told that you no longer performing the role of the member of the Historical Texts Advisory Board.

  • Name
  • Job Title
  • Organisation
  • Email address (general enquiries)
  • Telephone Number

As a member of the Historical Texts Advisory Board, we'll include your name, job title and affiliation in a public listing and meeting information that can be read from anywhere on the Internet. Please let us know if that will cause a problem, so we can discuss whether we can reduce what is displayed without devaluing communicating the work of the Advisory Board.

Example 3: A Historical Texts workshop which is hosted at an external venue

The following information is needed for us to reserve your place at the Historical Texts Workshop. We’ll use it, as described in our standard privacy notice (at https://www.jisc.ac.uk/website/privacy-notice), to provide the service you've requested, as well as to identify problems or ways to make the service better. We'll share this information with the workshop venue, so they can manage your attendance; our contract prohibits them from using the information for anything else. If you tick the 'include on delegate list' box, we'll also share your name and organisation with other delegates. If you ask us to keep your information on file for future events then we'll keep the information until we are told that you no longer want us to do so.

  • Name
  • Job Title
  • Organisation
  • Email Address
  • Telephone Number

Example 4: An individual participates in the Historical Texts User Satisfaction Survey and we are processing personal data

By providing information to the Historical Texts User Satisfaction Survey you agree that you have asked us to process it as described in our standard privacy notice (at https://www.jisc.ac.uk/website/privacy-notice). You may instruct us to stop processing it at any time by e-mailing historicaltexts@jisc.ac.uk or help@jisc.ac.uk with your email address: this will remove your data from future statistics and reports.
