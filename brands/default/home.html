The Burney Newspaper Collection

The Burney Newspaper Collection is part of Historical Texts. Historical Texts brings together Early English Books Online (EEBO), Eighteenth Century Collections Online (ECCO) 65,000 texts from the British Library 19th Century collection and the UK Medical Heritage Library (UKMHL).

The UK Medical Heritage Library and The Bristish Library 19th Century Collections are freely available. The rest of Historical Texts is available to UK HE and FE Institutions and Research Councils via subscription.

About the Burney Collection

The Burney Newspaper collection is a collection of seventeenth and eighteenth century news media and newspapers gathered together by the Reverend Charles Burney (1757-1817). In total the collection has over 1000 items including pamphlets, proclamations, newsbooks, periodicals and newspapers. There are nearly a million pages of content including some very rare items.

The labs status of this project

Historical Texts Labs are development sites created to test out new functionality or collections which the community can try out and feedback on. All subscribers to the Historical Texts service have access to this Lab.

This specific Lab site is for the Burney Newspaper collection, a new collection we will be adding to the Historical Texts service. The data structure for the Burney Newspaper collection is unlike the rest of the collections in Historical Texts, so it doesn't neatly fit into the main platform. However, we're developing the service to accommodate different format collections in future and this will be the first one.

This Lab is a first look at the Burney collection data with basic search and sorting options so further requirements can be gathered.

Feedback and requirements are welcomed and can be provided by email to the Historical Texts helpdesk
