Astronomica

Here we have a wide variety of texts charting the rapid development of astronomy over the late 17th century, inspired and driven by Galileo and others. We have texts by English astronomer Seth Ward, the first Astronomer Royal (John Flamsteed), and notable Dutch mathematician and astronomer Christiaan Huygens among others...
