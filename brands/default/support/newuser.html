New to Historical Texts

Here at Historical Texts we know that our users come to us from lots of different backgrounds and with different levels of research experience. We have made loads of help resources for you, but as a new user it can be a bit overwhelming knowing where to start. We hope that this selection of links will direct you to the help you need to get started using our service for the first time.

Please do contact our helpdesk at historicaltexts@jisc.ac.uk if you need any further help. We love to hear from our users and are happy to point you in the right direction if you get stuck.
