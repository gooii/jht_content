Frequently asked questions

Can i access Historical Texts?

Historical Texts is available via subscription to UK Higher Education and Further Education institutions and Research Councils who are full members of Jisc Collections. Eligible institutions can request a free 30 day trial of the resource. For any subscription enquiries please contact the Jisc Collections helpdesk at: help@jisc-collections.ac.uk.

Do you provide WAYFless URLs?

Yes, we provide WAYFless URLs for Historical Texts.

You can use the WAYFless URL generator (WUGEN) tool to generate one for your institition.

  • Go to the WAYFless URL generator (WUGEN) tool
  • Select your institution as the "Identity Provider"
  • Select "Historical Texts" as the Service Provider
  • Accept the default target URL of "https://historicaltexts.jisc.ac.uk/"

A WAYFless URL will be displayed for your institution for you to copy or email to yourself.

Are images and full text available for all collections?

Images are available for all three of our collections. Full text is available for ECCO, BL and for around a third of EEBO currently. Where full text is available "full text" will be clearly stated under the record on the results page. The data and features available varies by collection as each collection has been provided by a separate content provider. Details of these differences can be found on our features by collection page.

Can i download the texts?

Yes, you can download the titles in their entirety from all three collections in PDF format or individual images in JPEG format directly within the interface. Simply select a search result from the results list and open it in the viewer, then choose the download icon on the toolbar to download a PDF of the whole publication directly.

Please note that while PDFs for all three collections are available, only the PDFs for the BL collection are searchable and they have been provided by the British Library.
