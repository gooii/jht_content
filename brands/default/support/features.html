Features

These features are designed to highlight some of the wide variety of content available in Historical Texts. Direct links to texts and searches are included along with search tips to help you explore the resource further. We welcome suggestions for new features, as well as content to add to existing ones. So if you or your colleagues have ideas for features, or would like to write a feature yourself for us, please do contact our helpdesk at historicaltexts@jisc.ac.uk to let us know.
